The beautiful South Okanagan, where you can always find something to do.

There is always something to do during your stay at EZ RV Park. With an abundance of places to explore, you'll love your time in Canada's Wine Capital.

Whether you enjoy exploring nature parks, going fishing, relaxing while river tubing or hiking the beautiful local mountains, EZ RV Park will provide you the opportunity to choose your own adventure.
